About Kashmir

Kashmir has been the heart of fine pashmina craftsmanship for centuries. The region’s unique climate, skilled artisans, and deep cultural heritage give rise to textiles that are unmatched in softness, warmth, and beauty. Every shawl and gattara is a story of patience, handwork, and tradition passed down through generations.
